Favorite Project

My favorite project was working with Vault RMS and their CEO Chris Memmott. Vault RMS helps firefighters track on the job injuries and the application I worked on with Chris helped digitize the many handwritten forms firefighters would have to fill out. Our application allowed users to log into the platform and begin filling out forms digitally. The forms will be prepopulated with user data from a master key. This helps expedite the process for the user so he/she does not have to fill out redundant user data (name, address, birthday, etc.). It was great working with a passionate client and working on a project that had real-world application.
